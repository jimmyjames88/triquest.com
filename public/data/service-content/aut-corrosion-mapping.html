AUT and Corrosion Mapping

TriQuest NDT offers Automated Ultrasonics (AUT) for inspections and Corrosion Mapping; these applications can encompass many methods and techniques to fit our client's needs, industry specifications and site-specific requirements. Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT) and or Conventional Ultrasonics can be implemented to provide reliable rapid data collection. High sensitivity and resolution scans are achieved with encoded X-Y plotting of the inspected area ensuring accurate and repeatable data collection and analysis.

    Typical Applications for piping, tanks, and vessels include:

  • Wall Thickness Monitoring
  • Lamination Inspections
  • Corrosion Surveys
  • Corrosion Monitoring
  • High-Temperature Scanning Applications
  • Abrasion and Erosion Monitoring
  • Flow Assisted Corrosion
  • MFE Tank Floor Prove-Up
