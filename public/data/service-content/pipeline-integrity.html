Pipeline Integrity

Pipeline Integrity Assessments conform to industry standards, client specifications, and CSA Z662 – Oil and Gas Pipeline Systems. Inspection results complement and support our client’s pipeline integrity evaluation and rehabilitation in-line inspection programs. Conventional and advanced inspections are used to obtain the most accurate data for identification and evaluation of anomalies, correlation interpretation of indications and repair verification.

  • Coating assessments and inspections
  • Corrosion mapping - internal/external
  • Correlation and interpretation of ILI targets to as found conditions
  • Automated UT services (AUT)
  • Advanced NDT services (PAUT, TOFD)
  • Laser mapping
  • CWB visual inspection
  • GPS location
  • Soil Resistivity, Soil pH, Pipe to Soil Potential
  • Hardness testing
  • Defect assessment ID/OD (UT, MT, RT, PT, VT,)
  • Detection of deformations, mechanical damage, cracking, SCC, grinding repairs
  • Detection of long seam flaws, arc burns and manufacturer anomalies
  • PMI with OES
