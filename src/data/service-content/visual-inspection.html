Visual Inspection

Our visual inspection personnel are certified to perform quality assurance and quality control services to the Oil & Gas, Manufacturing and Power Generation industries. These services include plant turnaround planning, visual weld and dimensional inspection, and QA/QC document control for newly fabricated and in-service equipment.

Visual techniques are often used to compliment other NDT methods. The inspection tools include gauges, micrometers, calipers, magnifying devices, remote video equipment, and surface conditioning equipment to perform inspections.
